Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your schedule sorted.

How accurate is the AI scanning? expand_more
Our AI is trained specifically on Japanese school prints and newsletters. It achieves over 98% accuracy for standard formats. For complex layouts, you can easily edit the details before saving.
Is my data private? expand_more
Absolutely. We process images securely and delete them from our servers immediately after extraction. Your calendar data stays on your device or your chosen calendar provider (Google, Apple, etc.).
What's the difference between Free and Premium? expand_more
Basic allows for 5 scans per month and manual entry. Premium offers unlimited AI scans, cloud sync across devices, and the ability to share calendars with family members instantly.
Does it work with handwritten notes? expand_more
Currently, PicSche is optimized for printed text (newsletters, handouts, schedules). Handwriting recognition is in beta and available for Premium users to test, but accuracy may vary.
